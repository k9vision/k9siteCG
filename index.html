Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before getting started.

What training methods does K9 Vision use? expand_more

We use balanced, science-based training — combining positive reinforcement with clear, fair communication. Every plan is tailored to your dog's individual temperament and your specific goals, so the results last a lifetime.

What areas do you serve? expand_more

We proudly serve Houston, TX and the surrounding areas. Fill out our intake form with your location and we'll confirm availability and the best program for you.

How does the process get started? expand_more

Every program begins with a consultation and in-person evaluation. We assess your dog's specific needs, then present a customized training plan built for long-term success — with no obligation.

What dogs and behavior issues do you work with? expand_more

All breeds, ages, and temperaments. From puppy foundations and everyday obedience to behavioral rehabilitation (aggression, anxiety, reactivity), protection work, and scent detection — we build a plan around your dog.

How long does training take and what does it cost? expand_more

It depends on your dog and your goals — which is exactly what your evaluation determines. After the consultation we'll give you a clear timeline and pricing tailored to your situation, so there are no surprises.
